About

I’m motivated by product design and real-world impact. I can (and have) spent hours ensuring my applications are pixel-perfect, and I especially enjoy turning ideas into reality. I thrive on challenges and deadlines, am fanatical about the tiny details, and believe that thorough planning can save hours of programming.

Text Adventure

Text Adventure is a Ruby-based game where players wake up in a strange place, exploring and interacting with their environment in order to progress. I originally created this as a standalone Ruby program and later ported to the browser using Docker, Rack and websockets, enabling online gameplay. The game's modular code allows for easy expansion with new storylines, and its core classes—like Game, Player, Map, and Item—create a flexible framework for rich, text-based adventure.

Relister

Relister is a tool I built to simplify the process of relisting posts on secondhand selling platforms (currently supporting Craigslist and Facebook Marketplace). It features options for creating posts simultaneously across platforms and includes a price drop function, allowing users to relist ads with automatic price adjustments. In addition to its easy-to-use desktop interface, Relister includes a CLI for hands-off automation, supporting regular scheduling with cron jobs.

MacroMaker

MacroMaker is a mobile app I developed to simplify diet tracking by focusing on macro ratios and calorie management to help users achieve their fitness goals. Designed as an MVP, it offers core features like live tracking of daily intake, historical data, and average weekly insights, with alerts for calorie and macro targets. With an emphasis on minimal third-party services, MacroMaker delivers essential functionality directly on the device, providing a simple and efficient user experience for diet management.

  • Date: 2016-07-30
  • Category: React Native
